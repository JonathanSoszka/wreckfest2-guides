Testing Grounds is not a circuit and there is no race to win here. It is a sandbox packed
with stunt hardware — ramps, barrel stacks, a car cannon, crushers, jumps, and a set of
tuning-demo props — meant for driving around freely and seeing how the car reacts.

## What it is actually good for

Treat it as a place to feel changes rather than chase a time. Because nothing is at stake,
it is the ideal spot to:

- Learn a new car's limits — how it brakes, how quickly it rotates, how it lands off a jump —
  without a pack of rivals punishing every mistake.
- A/B a tuning change back to back. Make one adjustment on the [tuning guides](/tuning/),
  come here, and drive the same ramp or corner before and after to feel what actually moved.
- Get comfortable with contact and recovery — bumping objects, spinning, and gathering the
  car back up — in a low-stakes setting before it matters in a race.

## What to work on here

Everything you learn on the race tracks starts as a feel for the car, and this is the fastest
place to build that feel. When you change a setup and cannot tell whether it helped, bring it
here and isolate the one thing you changed. Then take it back to a real venue like
[FinnCross](/tracks/track01-finncross-circuit/) or [Savolax](/tracks/track02-savolax-sandpit/)
and confirm it under racing conditions.
